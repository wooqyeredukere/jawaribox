Our Story

What is Jawari?

In the villages of North Karnataka, when food is called "Jawari", it means it's authentic, original, and real.

We grew up eating kadak rotti made on clay stoves, chutney pudi ground on ancient stone grinders, and uppinakayi ripened under the summer sun. These weren't just meals — they were rituals passed down through generations.

JawariBox brings that authentic taste to your city apartment. Every item is prepared in our home kitchen using traditional methods and the finest ingredients. No shortcuts. No compromises.
